Our Vision

Bauhaus is a Creative PSD Template for Architecture & Interior.
Totally focused on tools to enhance and help your content shine. We have created this theme thinking only in options helpfully for the daily needs in a creative environment like an agency, a small studio or for the many freelancers out there seeking for a good looking portfolio but with coherent tools.

As a result working with hundred of creatives all these past years, we are now delivering a theme that will be part of your toolset for a lot of time, we are totally sure about this.

We also know that you´ll love Bauhaus as much as we do

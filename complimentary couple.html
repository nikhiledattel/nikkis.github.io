The positivity of differences

Synopsis

A couple become ideal not only when their likes are matching each others, but also when complementing each other.

I have heard once from a good family practitioner that, between an Ideal family couple, there exists upto 70% coherence and rest are differences. The ratio seems very realistic. Both are in one direction and aligned to each other for the chunk part, which obviously is essential. Now the rest portion is not just an undesirable part at all, but instead it is deliberately given to us so that it will become beneficial to other partner so that each need the other in order to thrive in life. One person may be a quick decision maker, but may have a bad temper at the same time, but then the other could be a little slow in deciding, but very patient and matured in emotional realm. They have all what can make them a perfect couple! Imagine otherwise, say both are short tempered, or both are slow! It could be really tragic isnâ€™t it? So we should change our perspective of seeing the differences and understand that they exist to become complementary to each other.

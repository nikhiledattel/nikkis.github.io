The scariest place to be in!

Synopsis

The confessional was once my scariest place and I felt too awkward to be there. But ever after I understood its meaning, it changed my perspective altogether

We’re all striving people, wanting to be better every day than it's previous one. As humans, we fall short many a times naturally. But we have to get up from there and run again. That's life. Spiritually too, this exercise is important.

In a catholic confession, there's a prayer called 'Act of Contrition', where one pledge to sin no more. So this ideally means that we don't have to make another confession ever! But you know what, in reality we are pledging that we will be back there any time soon, because we’ll fall again naturally.

A confessional is a place of knowing and loving yourself, in a deeper may. Again, this is a place of getting up and running again, leaving the past behind. Yes, its is a place of victory.

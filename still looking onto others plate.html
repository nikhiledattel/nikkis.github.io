Sometimes, the problem is that we aren't eating what's there in our plate

Synopsis

How does it be like gazing into other's plate when there's enough to satisfy us in our own plate. That sounds little awkward right? Well, this is not in fact about eating, but another charasteristic of ours, quite similar to this.

It is obvious that in order to do a thing, we need certain level of knowledge in that particular area. But it occurs that, sometimes we might actually be enough knowledgeable to do a thing, but still overthink and keep on researching undendingly. Ultimately, we will not be able to exectue something as and when required.

The problem could be that we aren't able to access the actual demand of the situation. If so, practice how to discern a situation and wisely put your knowledge into actions. Taking smaller steps and analysing/improvising could be a great way to gain confidence and gradually we'll be right back into the tune.

Please note

This shouldn’t be confused with a person’s honest quest of knowledge and trying to acquire it in highest level so as to better do something. The context here is the ‘over doing’ mentality or lack of judgement over what we know and what is actually required in executing a task.

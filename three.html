Sometimes, the problem is that we aren't eating what's there in our plate

How does it be like gazing into other's plate when there's enough to satisfy you in your own plate. This is true for some of us in the case of knowledge or idea.

We might be just enough knowledgeable to do something, but still we keep on searching for more. The actual reason why we can't execute those, is that we're not practicing what we know. Reading or knowing is only first step, and we need to become more of a doer. Start taking small steps, analyse the progress, improvise and repeat. Believe me, your job must have been done by now!

Please excuse

This is not about a true pursuit of knowledge and trying to acquire it in highest level so as to better do something, but about over analysing something or not understanding what's demand of a situation. I confess, I'm a struggling person too.
